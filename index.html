Unwind Mobile App Design

We discovered that many adults struggle to unwind about night, leading to late nights and poor sleep. Unwind was designed to help users form better unwinding habits before bed to promote healthier sleep.

U.S. Department of Education Redesign

My UX Bootcamp gave us the challenge of redesigning a government agency website. We selected the U.S. Department of Education due to its visually disorganized appearance and lack of clear, intuitive navigation. The website has an overwhelming amount of important information geared towards students, educators, and researchers in the education sector. Redesigning to clarify, visual organize, and restructure took many steps. Read on to see how we did it all.
